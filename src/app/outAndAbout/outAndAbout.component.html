There are quite a lot of things to do during your stay in Brevard.

  • Transylvania County is known for its waterfalls, including Sliding Rock.
  • There are a few breweries in Brevard:
    • Brevard Brewery
    • Sierra Nevada is about 30 minutes towards Asheville
    • Oscar Blues Brewery
  • Etowah Valley Country Club is a nice, local place to get some golfing in!
